The word chetna (चेतना) has multiple meanings, including consciousness, awareness, and animation. It can also be used as a verb to mean to become conscious, alert, or provoked.
Meaning
Consciousness: The state of being aware of one's thoughts, beliefs, and attitudes
Awareness: The state of being aware of one's surroundings
Animation: The state of being animated or alive
Intentions: In Buddhism, chetna refers to one of the 40 conditions associated with the mind
Usage
"Ideas came flowing into his consciousness"
"Cinema can change the consciousness of a nation"
Related words
Cetanā is an alternative spelling of chetna
Samskara is a Sanskrit word that means mental formation, action, conduct, or deed
